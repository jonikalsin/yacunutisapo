Raini Rodriguez - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, Single, Facts, Wiki

Table of Biography

Raini Rodriguez is a prominent actress, as well as a singer. Raini Rodriguez is famous for the characters named Maya in ‘Paul Blart: Mall Cop’, ‘Jessie’, ‘Prom’, ‘I’m in the Band’, and so on.

Early Life and Childhood

American actress, Raini Rodriguez was born on July 1, 1993, in Bryans, Texas. As of 2023 , she is 30 years old. She is of Mexican descent. Her father is Roy Rodriguez and his mother is Diane Rodriguez, who owns a business called Rodriguez Tire Service. She has three brothers; Rico Rodiguez, who is also an actor, Roy Jr, and Ray.

Caption: Raini Rodriguez with her brother Rico Rodriguez (Source: Hallmark Channel)

There is not very much for her education. She and her brother were homeschooled by her mother and she studied under booking coach Amy Lyndon. Moreover, her favorite TV show is ‘Friends’ and my favorite color is purple.

Professional Life

She appeared in the International Modeling & Talent Association (IMTA) Showcase when she was only eleven years old then talent agent Susan Osser noticed her performance, asked her mother to take on the role of her manager, and launched her career in showbiz. So she moved from Texas to Los Angeles, California with her mother and brother while his father and her other brothers stayed in Texas.

She played small roles in television and films till the time she got the break in the very first popular Disney Series that aired from 2011 to 2016, where she played the character named, Trish, in Austin and Ally.

Caption: Raini Rodriguez in Austin and Ally (Source: Hollywood Reporter)

Raini is also in her film career. She played the role of Maya Blart in the film ‘Paul Blart: Mall Cop’ in 2009, Tess Torres in ‘Prom’ in 2011, and Tavita in ‘Girl in Progress’ in 2012. There are many more to come to entertain us with her excellent acting skills.

She received the Images Award for Best Young Television Actress in the year 2013. Her breakout film role happened in 2009 with Paul Blart: Mall Cop. Later, she also co-starred with Eva Mendes in the 2012 film ‘Girl in Progress’. She has been awarded the Young Entertainer Award in the category of Best Leading Young Actress-Feature Film for Paul Blart: Mall Cop 2 in 2016 and nominated several times in her career.

As a singer, in 2012 she sang a song titled Living Your Dreams in English and Spanish for the movie Beverly Hills Chihuahua 3: Viva La Fiesta! Also, she featured in singles for Glee Mash-Up with Austin and Ally cast. Also sang promotional songs like You Wish You Were Me for Disney Channel Play It Loud, Double Take, and Row, Row, Row Your Boat for Austin and Ally.

2018-present

From 2018–2019, she voiced in the television show  The Lion Guard as Starehe. Likewise, in the same year, she appeared in Double Dare in the episode: “Team Rico vs. Team Raini.”  In 2019, she appeared in Squad as The Witch Doctor for the episode: “The Knight Stuff”, and voiced Maria Where’s Waldo for the Episode: “A New York Minute.”

In 2019-2020, she acted as Barb in the television series Bunk’d. Similarly, in 2020, she appeared in The Substitute for the episode: “Rico & Raini Rodriguez.”
