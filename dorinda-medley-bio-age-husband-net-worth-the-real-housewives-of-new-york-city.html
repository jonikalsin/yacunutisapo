Dorinda Medley Biography

Dorinda Medley is a popular Manhattan socialite and entrepreneur who is best known for her appearance on Bravo’s hit reality series, “The Real Housewives of New York City”. In addition, she founded her own cashmere company known as DCL Cashmere. Besides, she befriended celebrity clients like Princess Diana and Joan Collins.

Medley began her career and moved to London. After a while, she started her own cashmere company, DCL cashmere. After running the company for several years, she met several celebrity clients including Princess Diana and Joan Collins.

Afterward, Dorinda established herself as an independent millionaire entrepreneur. Together with her second husband, Richard, they worked in charity works raising funds for causes.

Besides, Medley got her career breakthrough after she was a cast member in a hit reality series, The Real Housewives of New York City in 2005. She appeared in the show from Season 7. Since then, she gained more fame and attention all over the country.

Similarly, she also appeared as a guest in shows like Watch What Happens, The Wendy Williams Show, Fablife, Hollywood Today Life. Furthermore, Medley launched her own accessories line by partnering with the popular branding company, Talent Resources.

Medley went to Franklin and Marshall College in Lancaster, Pennsylvania. She graduated in 1986, moved to New York, and started working in the showrooms of Liz Clairborne. Finally, she got to learn more about fashion and business. Over the years, she began her own business.

Dorinda Medley Age

How old is Dorinda Medley? Medley is 57 years old as of 2021. She was born on December 13, 1964, in Berkshires, Sandisfield, Massachusetts, the United States of America. She celebrates her birthday on the 13th of December every year. Additionally, her birth sign is Sagittarius.

Medley’s nationality is American, she belongs to White Caucasian ethnicity and is of Italian-Polish descent.

Dorinda Medley Husband

Initially, Medley was married to Ralph Lynch, an investment banker for ten years. Together, they have a daughter, Hannah Lynch. However, their marriage didn’t last, though she appears to be friendly with him as well as his wife.

In 2005, Medley married Richard Medley, a hedge fund adviser. However, in 2011, Richard died of liver failure. Currently, she is dating the owner of Madame Paulette, John Mahdessian.

Medley is blessed with a daughter, Hannah Lynch, from her first marriage with Ralph Lynch.
