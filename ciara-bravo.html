Ciara Bravo is an American actress who is popular for appearing in the television series Big Time Rush (2009-2013) and Red Band Society (2014-2015) and the YouTube series Wayne (2019).

Wiki/Biography

Ciara Bravo was born as Ciara Quinn Bravo [1] on Tuesday, March 18, 1997 (age 24 years; as of 2021), in Alexandria, Kentucky, U.S. Her zodiac sign is Pisces.

Childhood picture of Ciara Bravo with her elder sister, Rikkel Bravo

While talking about her childhood in an interview, she said,

Growing up in the Midwest, it never occurred to me that acting was a career choice. Previous to acting, I was taken by the idea of working with horses or the outdoors for a living.”

She attended a Catholic school named The Summit Country Day School. In April 2007, she was discovered by a talent scout at the Mike Beaty Model & Talent Expo at the age of nine. At Expo, she was the winner of the two-person scene for 8-12 years of age group and photography competition, and the first runner up in the runway modeling.

Physical Appearance

Height (approx.): 5′ 3″

Hair Color: Dark Brown

Eye Color: Black

Family

Her father, Mike Bravo, and her mother, Tammy Bravo, are owners of Executive Transportation Services, Inc. Limo Service in Newport. She has two siblings, an elder sister named Rikkel Bravo and a younger brother named Jaxon Bravo.

Ciara Bravo with her family

Career

Kentucky-born Ciara Bravo’s first paid acting job was to kiss a frog in the Newport Aquarium’s Frog Princess commercial.

In 2007, she gained recognition after starring in the Natasha Bedingfield music video of the song “Love Like This.”

Ciara Bravo in the music video of the song “Love Like This”

Voiceover

In 2008, she ventured into the entertainment business as a voiceover artist when she lent her voice in the animated series ‘Can You Teach My Alligator Manners?.’

Can You Teach My Alligator Manners? (2008)

She gave her voice in various projects including the television series Special Agent Oso (2009) and The Penguins of Madagascar (2012) and the television film Kinect Star Wars: Girly Vader (2012).

Film

Ciara Bravo began her career as a child actress with the 2009 American mystery thriller film ‘Angels & Demons’ in which she played the role of an Italian girl.

Angels & Demons (2009)

Thereafter, she appeared in various films like Neighbors 2: Sorority Rising (2016), To The Bone (2017), and The Long Dumb Road (2018). She rose to prominence with the 2021 American crime drama film ‘Cherry’ in which she played the role of Emily. The film is based on the autofictional novel of Nico Walker, an American author and US Army veteran who served time in prison for bank robbery. In the film, Nico, an Iraq War veteran suffering from PTSD is hooked on the Oxycontin prescribed for his anxiety. As the story unfolds, Nico begins to rob banks to feed his and his wife, Emily’s, drug addiction.

Ciara Bravo in Cherry (2021)

Television

She came into the limelight with her television debut in the American musical comedy series ‘Big Time Rush’ (2009-2013) as Katie, Kendall’s young, sarcastic, and mischievous sister who finds her life turned upside down when her brother’s big break forces the family to move to Los Angeles. Ciara earned the nomination for Best Performance in a TV Series – Supporting Young Actress at the Young Artist Awards (2012) for playing the role of Katie.

Ciara Bravo in Big Time Rush (2009)

She starred in the 2013 American fantasy-comedy television film ‘Jinxed’ as Meg Murphy, a young girl who is desperate to end a hundred-year-old family’s curse by offering her great-great-grandfather Tommy’s lucky coin to the descendant of a witch.

Jinxed (2013)

From 2014 to 2015, she gained massive popularity for playing the role of Emma Chota in the American teen comedy-drama series ‘Red Band Society.’ In the series, Emma Chota is a young brainy girl suffering from anorexia (an eating disorder), who is also the girlfriend of Leo and the love interest of Jordi.

Ciara Bravo in Red Band Society (2014)

In 2016, she appeared in the American science fiction crime drama ‘Second Chance’ in which she played the role of Gracie Pritchard, the granddaughter of the protagonist, Jimmy Pritchard (played by Robert Kazinsky). The storyline of the series revolves around Jimmy, a corrupt sheriff who was killed in a robbery at the age of 75. Through a little futuristic maneuvering, Jimmy is brought back to life by two billionaire tech geniuses.

Ciara Bravo in Second Chance (2016)

OTT/Web Series

In 2019, she struck stardom with the American action-comedy series ‘Wayne’ in which she played the female protagonist Delilah “Del” Luccetti. The YouTube series is about Wayne McCullough, a teenage boy who attempts to retrieve his late father’s stolen car along with his new crush Del.
