Sofia Richie Rocks Tie-Dye Bikini &amp; MatchesThe Blue WaterAtTheBeachOn Bahamas Vacay

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Sofia Richie is creating her very own bikini album from her countless swimsuit snaps while on vacation in the Bahamas! The model showed off her toned tummy in a tiny tie-dye suit by Frankies Bikinis in new photos!

Sofia Richie‘s swimsuit collection is everything and more. The model, 22, continues to share sultry bikini photos from her tropical vacation to the Bahamas with BFF Abby Smidt. In her latest Instagram uploads, shared on November 24,  Sofia looks stunning in a tiny two-piece by Frankies Bikinis — her go-to swimwear brand, which referred to Sofia as a “dream” in the comments.

Sofia’s aqua tie-dye bikini is the same two-piece the model wore in her Frankies Bikinis campaign photos back in the summer of 2019. The triangle top and string bottoms feature ruched adjustable material, so that it can be customized to reveal more or less skin. This particular print and style is still available on the brand’s website, along with a number of other tie dye styles.

In the new photos, Sofia’s picture standing in knee-deep, crystal clear waters. Her skimpy two-piece nearly matched the blue ombre ocean water behind her. The blonde beauty looked photoshoot ready, despite donning a fresh face and effortless, salty wet hair. Other snaps showed Sofia’s breathtaking vacation views, including a far-away photo that showed her lounging in the sand.

Sofia has been flaunting her fit physique on the beaches in the Bahamas since November 22. In a series of separate bikini snaps, she donned a tiny green swimsuit while she swam with “pigs in paradise.” After seeing the photos, Sofia’s famous friends were equally as impressed with her six-pack abs as we were.

“This body !!!,” Kylie Jenner wrote in the comments. DJ and model Chantel Jeffries added, “Ok fitness.” Yris Palmer, who recently spoke with HollywoodLife about how to maintain eyelash extensions while in quarantine, wrote, “Hello abs,” with a flame emoji. 

Sofia’s vacation comes just a week after her ex-boyfriend Scott Disick, 37, seemingly moved with model Amelia Hamlin, 19. Sofia unfollowed Amelia on Instagram after cozy photos of her ex and Lisa Rinna‘s daughter emerged this month. Scott and Amelia first stepped out together at a Halloween party on October 31.

Sofia and Scott split over the summer after three years of dating. The two first got together at the Cannes Film Festival in France in May 2017. Now, the model appears to be in the early stages of a romantic relationship with Cha Cha Matcha co-founder Matthew Morton, 27.
