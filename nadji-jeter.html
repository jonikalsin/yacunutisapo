Nadji Jeter also known as Nadji Anthony Jeter is an American actor, composer and musician. As of 2022, Nadji Jeter’s net worth is $3 million. He rose to prominence after his motion capture and a voice role in the 2013 action-adventure game and game of the year, The Last of Us. He then appeared in Marvel’s 2018 video game, Spider-Man: Miles Morales.

He is the recipient of the BTVA People’s Choice Voice Acting Award which he won under the Best Vocal Ensemble in a Video Game segment at 2014 Behind the Voice Actors Awards. He has acted in films such as The Fifth Wave (2016), Wonder (2017), The Runner (2021), Beats Style and Flavour (2006), Opposite Day (2009), etc.

He also acted in television series and shows such as Reed Between the Lines (2011), Everybody Hates Chris (2008), Last Man Standing (2015), etc. He also appeared in the series of Spider-Man games reprising his role as Miles Morales.
