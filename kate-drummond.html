Kate Drummond | Authority Phydra Actress, Utopia Falls Hulu Cast, Married, Husband, Net Worth, Famil

June 2024 · 5 minute read

Kate Drummond was born on 21st October 1975 in Woodstock, Ontario, Canada. She was raised in Ontario while her father served as a police officer, and mother worked at a milk factory. The actress went to St. Patrick’s High School prior to receiving a Bachelor’s degree in Kinesiology and Education.

Drummond was a full-time primary school teacher for 12 years in her hometown, where she only performed in local theatres. Later, she quit teaching and began pursuing a career as an actor. Kate is active in the acting industry since 2006 and is best known for Wyonna Earp (2016), Trapped: The Alex Cooper Story (2019), and Utopia Falls (2020).

Plays Authority Phydra on Utopia Falls Hulu (2020)

Utopia Falls is a dystopian sci-fi drama from Hulu, created by R.T. Throne and Joseph Mallozzi. The story is set 400 years in the future in a city called New Babyl. Interestingly, the city and the people residing there are divided into four sectors, known as Industry (builders), Progress (thinkers), Nature (growers), and Reform (troublemakers).

As per IMDb, the plot reads, "In a genre-bending take on a coming of age story, a group of teens in a distant future colony uncover an ancient forbidden archive of historical, cultural and musical relics. This discovery forces them to question everything they've been taught, and use the power of music to ignite change in their reality to expose the truth."

One of Drummond’s recognizable roles came in an independent drama film Room (2015) starring Brie Larson, where she featured as a neighbor in a scene with Jacob Tremblay. A year later, the actress wrote and directed the feature film Go Fish (2016), which was awarded Best Feature (Narrative) by the Muskoka Independent Film Festival.

Kate’s most notable credit includes the leading role as Nikki Bender in the Flower Shop Mystery television movie series, alongside famed actress Brooke Shields. Kate and Brooke’s characters are BFFs who run a flower shop together. The duo starred in all three movies; Flower Shop Mystery: Dearly Depotted (2016), Flower Shop Mystery: Snipped in The Bud (2016), and Flower Shop Mystery: Mum’s The Word (2016).

In 2015, the actress portrayed the character of Sienna Halvorson in a TV movie Lead with Your Heart, along with co-star Celeste Desjardins. This was followed by a recurring role as Agent Lucado for seven episodes on a supernatural western horror series Wynonna Earp, alongside Meghan Heffern.
